Top 10 places to visit in Rodrigues

Rodrigues, a hidden gem in the Indian Ocean, invites you to explore its enchanting beauty. Begin your journey with a visit to the picturesque Anse aux Anglais, a serene beach that offers tranquility and breathtaking sunsets. Dive into the island's history at the François Leguat Giant Tortoise and Cave Reserve, home to these ancient creatures. Nature enthusiasts will revel in Caverne Patate, a mystical cave system, and the panoramic views from Mont Limon. Discover the island's vibrant marine life while snorkeling in Trou d'Argent and exploring the coral reefs of Ile aux Cocos. Rodrigues, with its pristine landscapes and warm hospitality, promises a truly unique and unforgettable adventure.

January 15, 2021 / by Elizabeth Doe

Top 10 places to visit in Reunion Island

Reunion Island, a French paradise in the Indian Ocean, is a captivating blend of lush landscapes and cultural richness. Begin your journey at the striking Piton de la Fournaise, an active volcano with awe-inspiring hiking trails. Explore the vibrant markets of Saint-Denis, where local flavors and crafts abound. Adventurers will revel in the rugged canyons of Cirque de Mafate and Cirque de Cilaos, offering challenging treks and breathtaking vistas. Dive into the island's history at Maison Carrère and discover the unique Creole culture at La Saga du Rhum distillery. With its dramatic peaks, waterfalls, and warm hospitality, Reunion Island promises an unforgettable escape into nature and heritage.

Febuary 22, 2023 / by Avish Taukoorah

Top 10 places to visit in Maldives

The Maldives, a tropical paradise in the heart of the Indian Ocean, offers a dreamy escape. Start your journey at the luxurious overwater bungalows of the Maldives, where crystal-clear waters and white sandy beaches beckon. Dive into the vibrant underwater world at Maaya Thila and Banana Reef, perfect for snorkeling and scuba diving. For a taste of local culture, explore Malé, the capital city, with its bustling markets and historic mosques. Don't miss the surreal beauty of Bioluminescent Beach on Vaadhoo Island, where the shore shimmers with natural light. From sunset cruises to serene spa retreats, the Maldives is a haven for romance and relaxation, offering an unparalleled tropical experience.

Juin 23, 2023 / Tatiana Devis

Top 10 places to visit in Mauritius

Mauritius, a jewel in the Indian Ocean, beckons travelers with its diverse and captivating landscapes. Start your journey at the picture-perfect Trou aux Biches, where soft sands meet azure waters. Immerse yourself in the cultural vibrancy of Port Louis, the capital city, with its bustling markets and historic sites. For a dash of surrealism, visit the Chamarel Seven Colored Earths, a geological marvel. Nature lovers will be enchanted by Le Morne Brabant, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and the vibrant marine life of Blue Bay Marine Park. From the lush botanical gardens to the pristine Île aux Cerfs, Mauritius offers a tapestry of experiences for every traveler's delight.

January 15, 2023 / Emily Rose
